What We Do

We are a sustainable lumber company providing service both commercially, and to the public.

No matter the scope of your project, we have what you need.

We provide all types of cuts and states of wood. We also carry a wide array of tools.

We source whatever we can get our hands on. Live edge for small projects, exotics, all the way up to large timbers. Our shipment schedules are available by phone: (206) 291-7208.

Our lumber yards stock a wide variety of sheathing and panels.

There is a broad selection of fiberglass gypsum, plywood and underlayment.
